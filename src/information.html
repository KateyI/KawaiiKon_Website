Kawaii Kon

What Is Kawaii Kon?

Kawaii Kon is a 3-day conv- ention that celebrates Japanese anime (cartoons), manga (comics), and all facets of Japanese culture. We offer a variety of events and activities throughout the weekend. From video games, table top games, costume contests, thousands of manga to read, a talent-filled Artist Alley, and expansive Dealer’s Room, we have something for everyone!

Who Runs Kawaii Kon?

Kawaii Kon is operated by a core of directors from Hawaii in conjunction with a few mainland based directors. Kawaii Kon is a not-for-profit entity – that means no one makes a profit from the convention Not the owners, organizers or staff. We do it for the love of anime and the local otaku community!

Where is Kawaii Kon Held?

At the Hawaii Convention Center annually.

Is There an Age Requriment?

All ages are welcome at the convention! Children under 5 years old get in free, and ages 5-12 can enjoy the con at a lower child-rate for their membership. We do require that children under the age of 13 be accompanied by a parent or guardian. We also offer a few adult only events, where entry will require a government issued ID to verify age.

What Types of Events Are at Kawaii Kon?

Ask an Anime Character:

At this special event, attendeaes get the opportunity to ask their favorite characters almost any question.

Karaoke Competition:

If you like singing, and want to try your hand at competing, this is the contest for you! Join the Karaoke Competition and show what those pipes have got.

Fan Panels:

A place for fans to share their knowledge and charisma with the attendees of the convention!

VAST(Video Art Showcase Theater):

The Video Art Showcase Theater (or VAST for short) presents fan-made videos from all over the world. Fan-made videos are made by you, the fans!

Manga Library:

Need some time to just sit down and relax while reading your favorite manga? We have a large assortment of manga, covering all genres, so there is something for everyone!

Video Rooms:

An anime convention is not complete without video rooms! Kawaii Kon has three video rooms offering the best titles available for all your anime viewing needs.

Table Top Gaming

The Tabletop Gaming room provides a place for gamers and non-gamers to play their old-favorites or learn a new game, purchase tabletop related merchandise, or enter a tournament and win prizes.

Video Games:

we strive to bring you the very best in the latest consoles, and games. Additionally we host tournaments throughout the weekend for you to test your skills against other Kawaii Kon gamers!

Cosplay Events

The Hallway Cosplay Contest lets fellow kon-goers vote for their favorite Cosplay. Or compete with the best of the best costumers at Kawaii Kon at the Cosplay Masquerade.
